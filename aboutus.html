Who We Are

We're avid water lovers who want to get on the water!

Norfolk Residents

Many of us live in the Ghent neighborhood of Norfolk, Virginia. We want a boat launch fo Norfolk residents as well as visitors.

Water Lovers

We love the water and the many opportunities to enjoy the water in Norfolk. We look forward to sailing out to watch sunsets on the Elizabeth River and paddling out to watch the fireworks during Harborfest or 4th of July celebrations.

Dedicated Community Activists

We are eager to continually enhance our city with smart, deliberate, and reasonable improvements. We love Norfolk and particularly the Ghent neighborhood and support this as one of the many improvements that make this the best neighborhood in Hampton Roads.
